The Human Cost of Silence
Imagine missing your child's first words — not because you weren't there, but because the world wasn't built for you.

Over 466 million people worldwide experience disabling hearing loss. Existing solutions — bulky hearing aids, phone-based apps, expensive cochlear implants — force a choice between presence and participation. Vaakya was built to end that choice.

The Technology

Three layers.
One seamless experience.

Vaakya is built from the ground up as a wearable system — not a retrofitted app. Every feature was designed to vanish into your natural field of vision, putting you back in the conversation.

Real-Time Captions
Spoken words appear as overlaid text in your visual field within milliseconds. Powered by on-device Vosk ASR — no cloud dependency, full privacy.
<80 ms end-to-end latency
Emotional Sentiment
Words carry tone. Vaakya layers real-time sentiment signals — joy, concern, urgency — so you catch the subtext, not just the text.
6 emotional dimensions tracked
Speech Amplification
Adaptive directional audio enhancement picks out the voice you're focused on — filtering background noise so the room comes to you, not the other way around.
3× signal-to-noise improvement
